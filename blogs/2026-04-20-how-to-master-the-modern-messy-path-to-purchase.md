An RTB House consumer study reveals that modern shoppers start their purchase journey with careful consideration rather than immediate intent, visiting sites multiple times and comparing numerous options before buying. The research shows 78% of fashion consumers evaluate at least three options, with Gen Z taking significantly longer than older generations to complete purchases, indicating that traditional promotional calendars no longer guarantee conversions.
