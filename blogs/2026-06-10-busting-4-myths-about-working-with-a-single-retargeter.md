RTB House's Jaysen Gillespie argues that using multiple retargeting partners outperforms single-vendor strategies, challenging claims from legacy machine learning providers. The article debunks four myths: that most clients use one retargeter, that multiple partners increase auction costs, that audience overlap hurts performance, and that multiple providers cause frequency blindness.
