Head-to-head testing compares advertising vendors directly by splitting audiences equally, but it overlooks critical synergies in multi-vendor strategies. Incrementality testing, which measures true causal lift through control groups, provides a more complete picture of each partner's actual contribution, and the article argues it represents the gold standard for assessing vendor effectiveness.
